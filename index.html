About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Geotechnical Engineering with a research focus on the application of Artificial Intelligence to soil mechanics. My work integrates interpretable machine learning models such as SHAP, XGBoost, and PSO-MLP hybrids to analyze and predict soil compressibility and related properties.
